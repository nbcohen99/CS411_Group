Welcome to Bill the Split




A web app that allows a user to easily split a bill and bill the split

BTS provides an easy solution to splitting costs from a group outing starting with the car ride to the restaurant to the check at the restaurant. It utilizes the Yelp API by taking user input filters and providing local restaurant recommendations, and ranks them based on estimated travel time and cost pulled from the Lyft API. A user can then push Venmo pay requests to members of the group for the car ride, and after their meal, do the same for the check.
